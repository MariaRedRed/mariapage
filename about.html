Hello there!

My name is Maria Red, I'm a multidisciplinary Visual Development / Concept Artist, Designer, Background/Layout Artist and a WIZARD working for Animation, Videogames and Illustration industries.

I've worked in the entertainment industry since 2015 for different projects and companies; Visual Development and Color Script Artist for the 3D TV Animated Series Treasure Trekkers (B-Water Studios), making the Concept Art for the indie game based in Greek mythology Pandora: The Chains of Chaos, and in the most recent years as Background/ Layout Artist for several productions like The Cuphead Show!, by NETFLIX.

Currently I'm Senior BG/Layout Artist at the Emmy and Annie nominated animation studio Lighthouse Studios for Rick and Morty S7.

I've had the opportunity to work with clients such as Amazon, NETFLIX or Green Portal Productions.

Originally from Spain, due to my job I move a lot, but currently I am based in beautiful Kilkenny, Ireland where I work In-House and also remotely.

Always open to work in new exciting projects and adventures. If you are in need of a creative with my set of skills or you have any ideas for a potential creative collaboration, please feel free to send me a word and I would be happy to discuss them.

You can reach me anytime here: art.mariared@gmail.com
