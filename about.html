Hello there!

My name is Maria Red, I'm a multidisciplinary Visual Development / Concept Artist, Background/Layout Artist, Illustrator and a WIZARD working for Animation, Videogames and Illustration industries.

I've worked in the entertainment industry since 2015 on different roles, projects and companies; Visual Development/Color Script Artist for the 3D TV Animated Series Treasure Trekkers (B-Water Studios), Layout Artist for The Cuphead Show!, Lighthouse Studios (NETFLIX) and Lead Layout Artist on Rick&Morty S7/S8 by Green Portal Productions (Adult Swim), Concept Artist for Lightbox Animation Studios, Freelance Illustrator for 'Call of Cthulhu' and 'Lords of the Middle Sea' RPG by publisher Chaosium.Inc, and Senior Concept Artist at Arxlight Pictures working on the Feature Monstrous MIA among other projects.

I've had the opportunity to work with clients such as Amazon, NETFLIX, Apple TV or Green Portal Productions.

Originally from Spain, but currently I'm based in beautiful Kilkenny, Ireland, where I work as a Freelance Artist, In-House and remotely.

Always open to work in new exciting projects and adventures. If you are in need of a creative with my set of skills or you have any ideas for a potential creative collaboration, please feel free to send me a word and I would be happy to discuss them.

You can reach me anytime here: art.mariared@gmail.com
